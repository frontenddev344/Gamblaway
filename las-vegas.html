The legal gambling age in Las Vegas is 21 years old. This applies to all types of gambling, including casino games, sports betting, and poker.

Las Vegas offers various gambling options, including slot machines, table games (such as blackjack, craps, and roulette), sports betting, and poker. Visitors can also participate in casino tournaments and other special events.

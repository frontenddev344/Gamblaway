Atlantic City offers a range of gambling options, including table games like blackjack and roulette, slot machines, poker rooms, sports betting, and various entertainment and dining choices.

Complimentary drinks are typically provided to gamblers actively playing in Atlantic City's casinos, but policies can vary by casino. It's advisable to check with the specific casino for their drink service details.

Atlantic City casinos generally have a casual to smart casual dress code. While some venues may require more formal attire, respectful behavior and following table etiquette are crucial for an enjoyable experience.
